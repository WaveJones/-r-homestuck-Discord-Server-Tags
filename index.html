+t shitscore

You put in +t shitscore [PERSON 1] [PERSON 2] [OPTIONAL PERSON]. And then @42 will output this:

=====SHITSCORING===== [PERSON 1]^ (THE QUADRANT)^^ *[PERSON 2]^^^

(THE BOT'S COMMENT)^^^^ =====SHITSCORING=====

^This line will be the [PERSON 1] you put earlier. If you put nothing for the [PERSON 1], the bot will generate a random user for you. ^^This line will be the QUADRANT which defines the shitship. ^^^This line will be the [PERSON 2] you put earlier. If you put nothing for the [PERSON 2], the bot will take your username instead. ^^^^This line will be the COMMENT on the ship.

====NOTES!!!==== The [PERSON]s can't be multi-worded, unless ?ou put quotes around them. [OPTIONAL PERSON] is only used for Auspisticism (or as 42 calls it, Ausspississm), you can leave it blank, it will generate a random user.

====EXAMPLE==== +t shitscore AndrewHussie The bot will reply with: =====SHITSCORING===== AndrewHussie {set:skore;{choose:? 'Kismitude';? 'Maspritship';? 'Moralliance';? 'Aussipississm' The Asshole Between Them -={user}=-;};} {skore}

Your Username

14% Where'd you get this ship? The thrift store? =====SHITSCORING=====

+t blackmarket

Just put in +t blackmarket [THING] in and it'll score how much [THING] is worth on the black market.
